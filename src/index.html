About New Leaf

At New Leaf Landscapes & Lakeshores, we bring over 25 years of experience creating beautiful, functional, and environmentally responsible outdoor spaces across Michigan. From riprap rock seawalls and erosion control to lakeside patios and outdoor living areas, we specialize in protecting and enhancing waterfront properties.

Our journey began on a hog farm in Southwest Michigan—where the value of hard work was learned early. That foundation, paired with a degree in environmental science, drives our passion for sustainable shoreline solutions. We use industry-best practices and a proven process to ensure every project is completed on time, on budget, and with care.

Lakeside Landscapes

At New Leaf Landscapes & Lakeshores, we specialize in designing and building high-quality lakeside landscapes that enhance your shoreline while protecting your investment. From flagstone patios and boulder retaining walls to custom outdoor living spaces, our team creates beautiful, functional environments tailored to your lakefront lifestyle.

We bring over 25 years of experience in shoreline landscaping, combining creative design with erosion control and shoreline stabilization best practices. Every project is built to withstand Michigan’s changing seasons and fluctuating water levels. Whether you need a new rock bed, hardscape installation, or a full lakeside renovation, we deliver lasting results that increase property value and curb appeal. Trust New Leaf to turn your shoreline into a stunning outdoor retreat that reflects your vision and stands the test of time.
